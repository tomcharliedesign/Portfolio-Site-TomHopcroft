About me

My Story

I’m a UI designer with a background in account management in tech start-ups. I have experience solving user problems in fast paced environments and creating niche solutions for users in budget constrained environments. This led me to look at the products I worked with in great detail and inspired me to create functional and simple designs that support the product rather than get in the way of it. I now create intuitive and clean designs that serve the product and ultimately the user.
